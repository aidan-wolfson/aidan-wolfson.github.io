Carlton Cinema Accessibility

At Carlton, we believe that the magic of movie-going is for everyone. That's why we're committed to making our theatres more accessible and enjoyable for guests of all abilities. From sensory-friendly screenings to reserved spaces for those requiring mobility devices and their companions, to assistive technology for those who are deaf, hard of hearing, blind, or visually impaired and more, our mission to make the movies as inclusive as possible is an ongoing priority.

We're proud to ensure our guests enjoy a quality movie-going experience, and you can discover more with our initiatives below.

Sensory-Friendly Screenings

All ages program for individuals with autism spectrum disorders

Carlton is proud to present Sensory-Friendly Screenings for all ages. This program provides a sensory-friendly environment for individuals with autism spectrum disorders and their families to view newly released films in a lights up, sound down environment in theatres across the country.
